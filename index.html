FAQs

What is World of Wizards?

+

World of Wizards is a unique questing p2e game on the Solana blockchain.

What is the supply?

+

The supply is 999.

What is the mint price?

+

0.99 SOL.

When is the mint date/time?

+

2 Mar 2022 17:00:01 UTC

Is the mint WL only?

+

We plan on the mint being whitelist only but we are going with the old school FCFS system. We don't want inflated WL token prices being sold on token marketplaces causing unrealistic expectations and investors being burnt. If WL happens to not sell out (very unlikely but still a possibility), we will be releasing the mint for public. I highly recommend having your alarm ready.

When will the game be released?

+

We are releasing a demo of the game no more than a couple hours after we have sold out. This will give investors the ability to test out their NFTs and start levelling up their character.

Is there a token and staking mechanism?

+

There sure is. We plan to roll out the token $SPELL and staking the following days after launch. This will allow investors to generate passive income through staking their Wizards as well as earning the token within the game.

Will there be future collections that are released?

+

We have contemplated releasing future collections but it is not a priority. We aim to make the gameplay + staking experience as fun and smooth as possible before we worry about launching additional collections. This is the ultimate goal of the team to make the experience as wholesome and exciting as possible for investors.
