The Bored Zombie Yacht Club

We have an amazing linear roadmap and plans for the future not just for this year but we are planning to transition smoothly into 2023 and beyond! - we are here and we are here to stay!

Fresh launch with a group of developers, marketers and business advisors simultaneously drawing up initial plans and ideas to bridge the gap between celebrities and fans with our project thus constructing utility that our NFTs hold and also at the same time garnering exclusivity.
Brand partnerships shall develop to take this into the next realm - planting a lot of our money into prizes for those whom mint specific Zombies, you could receive a Signed Collectible, Electronic, Car or even a Rolex if you're really lucky! - we're trying to do as much as we can to compete for the number one spot on Solsea!

We see many projects try to attempt a very intricate pre-sale however with BZYC we plan to giveaway white-list spots to active and deserving community members | After the private sale we will post the public sale where the rest of the Discord will have a chance to mint 6 Zombies per wallet (bear in mind there are only 6666 zombies!)

The onboarding of top celebrities will begin and plans to overtake the Solana NFT World will start to begin (this is expanded in Phase 3)
PHASE 3
Launch of the BZYC own Metaverse World (this has been a fundamental idea from the initial conceptions of this project) also introducing the framework for a App that will be available to both Apple & Android users to interact with each other on the Yacht and owners of the App will recieve exclusive news, invitations to events and more (club exclusive).
Project fund wallet will be continuously used to cover expenditures of the project ensuring it's longevity and it's transition into 2023 and beyond. We will continue purchasing land in the Metaverse, developing exclusive deals with artists, celebrities and influencers. We will also expand onto other NFTs and expand the company whilst solidifying the exclusivity of our Bored Zombies and cement it's monumental contribution not just in the realm of NFTs but for the Metaverse and the Solana Blockchain.

Towards the fall of 2022 we will announce our new animated series - which will come into fruition mid 2023. We have already contacted and established networks with a animation studio where your Zombies will come to life in a animated series! -> During this point we will have already sold out all Zombies and the exclusive owners of the Zombies will get a chance to voice their own Zombies (we at this point plan to work with celebrities and major influencers!) Bored Zombies Yacht Club is here to stay and is giving an equal opportunity to all those who wish to join us on this journey, we are here and we are here to stay
